Improving the Way Developers and Planners Assess Parking Demand

We Collected Overnight Residential Parking Data from Nearly 200 Apartment and Condo Buildings Throughout Metro Boston

Here is what we found

  • Three out of ten spaces sit empty during peak demand
  • The key factors that drive parking demand are parking supply, transit accessibility and the percentage of deed-restricted units
